# ⚡ Quick Start Guide

## 30 Seconds to Launch

### Step 1: Open the File
Double-click **`start_here.html`** for the welcome page, or open **`index.html`** directly in your browser.

### Step 2: Explore
Scroll down to discover 139 interactive sections. Tap, click, and play your way through the Islamic Golden Age.

### Step 3: That's It!
No install. No server. No account. Everything works offline after first load.

---

## 🌐 Change Language
Click the language switcher in the top-right corner:
- **EN** — English (default)
- **FR** — Français
- **عربي** — Arabic (switches to right-to-left layout)

Your choice is saved automatically.

---

## 🌟 Tips
- **Earn stars** by completing activities — they're tracked in the top-right corner
- **Use the navigation** drawer (☰ menu) to jump to any section
- **Press S** to search sections, **T** to jump to top
- **Enable sound** for click effects and narration
- **Come back daily** for new challenges and to keep your streak going
